AASHRI SOCIETY is a not-for-profit organization

AASHRI SOCIETY is a not-for-profit organization operational since 2009 and was registered under the AP Registrations Act as a Society (NGO). Aashri Society is based in Hyderabad, Telangana. In Sept 2014, AASHRI established a Children's Home near IDA Bollaram, Hyderabad with 45 children both 19 boys and 16 girls. Recently, we moved to new premises at Mayuri Nagar, Miyapur with 35 boy kids.

We currently support 360 children - 191 boys and 169 girls in the age group 6-19 in 6 Mandals of khammam district. Out of these, we have 35 children in our orphanage located at Miyapur and the rest of the children are in various schools in parts of Telangana. In addition, we sponsor school fees of 244 more children who live in the vicinity and whose parents cannot afford education for their children. We make the fee payment directly to schools through sponsors that these children attend.

We currently employ 6 people to take care of cooking, tuition and security of our premises.
