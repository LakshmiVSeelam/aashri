The Journey

Journey

Since 2011

Aashri Society started its journey during, my college days in the year 2011 | started with street children of 19 boys and 45 girls providing basic education and nutritional food at papireddy colony chandanagar as education is one of the most effective tools that can eradicate poverty and strengthen our nation as they lack opportunities while as aashri society was registered in the year 2014 July 19.

The Challenge

Challenge

We rented a very modest house in IDA Bollaram to start our Children’s Home. At the end of the first yedr, we had about 45 children and with a very little support. There are a lot of kids who lost parents and are in need of food, education, clothes, health care and accommodation. Without any external support, these children do not see any future for themselves and end up as beggars, rag pickers and even victims of child trafficking. We wish to provide them a safe and secure childhood filled with love and care. This will help the children to transform themselves into able citizens of the nation. The initial days were those of lack of resources, insecurity and instability. Most people did not trust our intentions or our abilities. We had little money to fund our vision and dreams.

At the same time, we could not abdicate our responsibilities and run away from challenges. We took solace from the fact that most organizations have a humble beginning and our case will be no different, we would get there someday. We started sending our children to an English Medium Private School in the vicinity.

In 2015, we started getting support from local businessmen, employees of various companies and students. There was steady flow of volunteers from colleges and financial support from few of the corporates in Hyderabad, Through this support, we are providing them nutritious food, planning to start library and computer lab and ample recreation facilities Soon, Aashri Society started getting visibility in the social sector. We were featured in and interviewed by all Telugu,English and daily newspapers and majority news channels.

In academics, some of our children have stood among the toppers in their schools. We have children who passed out of High School one even finished her medicine some finished their masters while as few kids who completed their Diploma in ITI went to abroad through Aashri society and few are still pursuing their higher education.

It is the sincerity and dedication of our staff members along with the generosity of our friends and well-wishers that provides our children an opportunity to carve their own destinies. Our children cherish their lives at AASHRI and look forward to a great future. Your support and patronage will encourage our children to dream big and succeed.

The Impact

Impact

Most children before coming to AASHRI were in government schools or no Schooling. The schools in remote area are pathetic. Students are promoted to higher classes purely based on their attendance and not necessarily on the basis of their performance in examinations.To Boost their Confidence Some of our kids used to work on fields during the harvest season and some to rear cattle, skipped classes for the better part of the academic year. Our immediate challenge is to make the kids oriented towards studies and make them understand the importance of the education in building their careers and lives.

Ever since we started taking comprehensive care of these children, we have been pursuing one single goal — make them good citizens of the nation. Accordingly, all the basic needs are being met on par with other children who have a proper family backing. We provide them good food, clothing and recreation facilities.
